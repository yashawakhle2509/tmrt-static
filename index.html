Desilting Wells

We specialize in desilting wells,
which involves removing
accumulated silt and debris from
wells. Our skilled team uses the
latest technology and equipment
to ensure efficient and effective
desilting, resulting in a steady and
clean water source for farmers.

Water Accessibility

farmers to maintain healthy
crops.

Why Choose TMRT ?

The Marine Robotics Team (TMRT) is a dedicated group of individuals
with a strong mission to make a positive impact in the field of
agriculture. Our primary goal is to address critical issues that affect
farmers and their livelihoods. We strive to provide innovative solutions
to transform the agricultural landscape and improve the well-being of
farming communities.

 The increasing rates of farmer suicides, driven by low harvests and
insufficient income, deeply affect farming communities. We are
dedicated to preventing such tragic outcomes by improving
agricultural productivity, helping farmers escape the cycle of debt,
and providing a sustainable future. By addressing these challenges,
we hope to reduce the economic and emotional burden on farming
families.
